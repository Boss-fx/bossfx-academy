Our Story

The Real Story Behind
BossFx Academy

In 2022, I deposited my savings into a live Forex account. Three weeks later, it was gone. No stop loss. No risk management. No plan. I was a demo millionaire who couldn't survive a single month of real trading.

I looked for help and found the same thing everywhere: Telegram signal groups charging monthly fees, "mentors" flashing rented cars, and courses that taught you to draw trendlines but never mentioned position sizing. The entire education system was broken — especially for traders in West Africa.

So I stopped looking for a teacher and became my own. I studied Computer Science, spent 4+ years deep in the markets, and eventually started coding my own trading bot in MQL5. MetaQuotes rejected my first submission with 21 validation errors. I fixed every single one. The BossFx SMA Pro Trend EA is now live on the MT5 Market — validated, published, and trading the way I designed it.

BossFx Academy is the education I wish existed when I started. No fluff, no hype — just the structured path from confused beginner to confident, disciplined trader. Every lesson is one I paid for with real money and real losses.
