## Content Production Workflow

### Step 1: Ideation
- Check trending topics on TikTok and Instagram
- Review keyword research for blog topics
- Note questions from Telegram community
- Check competitor content for gaps

### Step 2: Creation
- Write script/outline (or use Content AI)
- Record video / create graphics
- Write captions with CTA
- Add relevant hashtags

### Step 3: Optimization
- Instagram: Relevant hashtags (15-20), location tag
- TikTok: Trending sounds, relevant hashtags
- YouTube: SEO title, description, tags, thumbnail
- Blog: JSON-LD, meta tags, internal links, OG tags
- X: Engagement hooks, thread structure

### Step 4: Publishing
- Post at optimal times (generally 7-9 AM WAT for Nigerian audience)
- Cross-post where appropriate
- Share in Telegram community
- Engage with comments for first 30 minutes

### Step 5: Repurposing
```
YouTube tutorial (20 min)
  ├── TikTok clip 1 (key tip, 30s)
  ├── TikTok clip 2 (interesting moment, 30s)
  ├── Instagram Reel (highlight, 60s)
  ├── Instagram Carousel (key points, 5-7 slides)
  ├── X Thread (main lessons, 5-7 tweets)
  ├── Blog post (expanded written version)
  └── YouTube Short (best clip, <60s)
```

One long-form piece = 7+ short-form pieces.

## Content Calendar Template

### Monthly Planning (Last week of prior month)
1. Choose 4-5 main topics for the month
2. Map topics to content pillars
3. Assign to weekly slots
4. Identify repurposing opportunities
5. Note any product launches or promotions

### Content Batch Day (1x per week)
1. Create 5-7 social media posts in one sitting
2. Record 2-3 videos in one session
3. Write captions and hashtags for all
4. Schedule using platform native tools or drafts
5. Plan engagement strategy for each post

## AI-Assisted Content Production

### Content AI Can Create
- Blog post drafts (SEO-optimized)
- Social media captions and hashtags
- Email copy (drip sequences, campaigns)
- Video scripts and outlines
- Content calendar recommendations
- Repurposing suggestions
- SEO keyword research

### Content AI Cannot Create
- Video recordings (founder must record)
- Personal stories and experiences
- Live trading analysis
- Student testimonials (must be real)
- Live mentorship sessions

### AI Content Workflow
1. CEO provides topic or keyword
2. Content AI creates draft
3. CEO reviews, adds personal touches
4. CEO records video (if applicable)
5. Content AI optimizes captions, tags, metadata
6. CEO publishes
