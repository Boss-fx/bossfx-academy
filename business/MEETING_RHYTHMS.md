# Meeting Rhythms — BossFx Academy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-30
> **Owner:** CEO
> **Context:** Solo founder operation — "meetings" are structured self-reviews and AI working sessions

---

## Why This Exists

Solo founders skip reflection because there's no one to meet with. These rhythms replace meetings with structured reviews that force the same accountability a team would provide.

---

## Daily Stand-Up (5 min, every morning)

**Format:** Self-review before starting work

**Questions:**
1. What did I accomplish yesterday?
2. What are the top 3 priorities today?
3. Is anything blocked?

**Output:** Mental clarity on the day's focus. No document required.

---

## Weekly Business Review (Saturday, 15 min)

**Format:** Dashboard review + written notes

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Open Admin Dashboard → orders, revenue, EA addon rate
- [ ] Open GA4 → sessions, top pages, traffic sources
- [ ] Check Brevo → email sends, open rates
- [ ] Check Telegram → member count, active conversations
- [ ] Check social media → follower growth, top-performing content
- [ ] Scan Vercel logs → any errors this week?
- [ ] Note: What worked this week?
- [ ] Note: What didn't work?
- [ ] Set 3 priorities for next week

**Output:** Brief notes for personal reference. Update KPI_DASHBOARD.md monthly with trends.

---

## Monthly Business Review (1st Saturday of the month, 1 hour)

**Format:** Full review with Claude Code session

**Agenda:**
1. **Revenue review** (15 min)
   - Total revenue this month vs last month
   - Revenue by product
   - EA addon conversion rate
   - Refund count (if any)

2. **Growth review** (15 min)
   - New leads captured
   - Email list growth
   - Social media follower growth
   - Traffic trends (GA4)
   - Organic search performance

3. **Product review** (10 min)
   - Orders per product
   - Customer feedback (Telegram, email)
   - Support issues pattern
   - Product improvement ideas

4. **Content review** (10 min)
   - Content published this month
   - Best-performing content
   - Content gaps
   - Next month's content plan

5. **System health** (5 min)
   - Vercel: function count, error rate
   - Supabase: database size, storage usage
   - Brevo: email delivery rate, daily send count
   - Any security concerns

6. **Action items** (5 min)
   - Top 3 priorities for next month
   - Roadmap items to tackle
   - Update CHANGELOG.md if releases shipped

**Output:** Update KPI_DASHBOARD.md. Update PROJECT_ROADMAP.md if priorities shifted.

---

## Quarterly Strategy Session (1st week of quarter, 2 hours)

**Format:** Deep review with Claude Code session

**Agenda:**
1. **Quarter in review** (30 min)
   - Revenue total and trend
   - Customer count and growth
   - Top wins and losses
   - Roadmap items completed

2. **Competitive landscape** (15 min)
   - What competitors are doing
   - Market trends in forex education (Nigeria)
   - Pricing comparison

3. **Product strategy** (30 min)
   - Product performance (keep/kill/improve)
   - Pricing adjustments needed
   - New product opportunities
   - Value ladder effectiveness

4. **Growth strategy** (20 min)
   - Channel effectiveness
   - New channel experiments
   - Partnership/collaboration opportunities
   - Paid advertising readiness

5. **Operations** (15 min)
   - What to automate next
   - Technical debt to address
   - Infrastructure upgrades needed
   - Documentation gaps

6. **Goal setting** (10 min)
   - Revenue target for next quarter
   - Growth target (leads, subscribers, followers)
   - 3 strategic priorities
   - Roadmap for next quarter

**Output:** Update PROJECT_ROADMAP.md. Update this Business OS if strategy changes.

---

## Annual Planning (December, Half day)

**Format:** Full business review + next-year planning

**Agenda:**
1. Year in review — revenue, growth, milestones
2. What worked — keep doing
3. What didn't work — stop or change
4. Market assessment — where is the opportunity
5. Annual revenue target
6. Product roadmap for the year
7. Infrastructure investments needed
8. Skills to develop
9. Monthly revenue milestones
10. Update all Business OS documents

**Output:** Annual plan document. Updated roadmap. Updated KPI targets.

## AI Working Sessions (As Needed)

**Format:** Claude Code sessions for specific projects

**Types:**
| Session Type | Duration | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Bug fix / incident | 15-30 min | Diagnose and fix production issues |
| Feature build | 1-2 hours | Implement new functionality |
| Documentation update | 30-60 min | Keep Business OS current |
| Code review / audit | 1-2 hours | Quality and security review |
| Strategy discussion | 30-60 min | Product/growth/marketing decisions |

**Protocol:** Every AI session should read CLAUDE.md first, check PROJECT_ROADMAP.md, and document work in CHANGELOG.md.
