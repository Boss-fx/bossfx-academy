# Mission & Vision — BossFx Academy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-30
> **Owner:** CEO

---

## Mission

Make professional forex trading education and automation tools accessible to every serious trader in Africa.

---

## Vision (3-Year)

By 2029, BossFx Academy is the most trusted fintech education platform in Africa — known for validated tools, honest education, and measurable student outcomes. The platform runs autonomously with AI-powered operations, serving thousands of students across multiple African markets with courses, mentorship, proprietary EAs, and a mobile app.

---

## Core Beliefs

1. **Trading is a skill, not a gamble.** It can be taught with structure, validated with data, and improved with discipline.
2. **Technology should serve the trader.** The SMA Pro Trend EA exists because automation removes emotion from execution. Every tool we build solves a real trading problem.
3. **Africa deserves professional platforms.** Nigerian traders shouldn't have to settle for WhatsApp groups and unverified "gurus." We build to the same standard as any global fintech company.
4. **Honesty over hype.** No "get rich quick" promises. No fake screenshots. We show our EA's MetaQuotes validation. We show real curriculum. We show real results.
5. **Build once, operate forever.** Every system we build is documented, automated, and designed to run without constant human intervention.

---

## North Star Metric

**Paying students who complete their program and return for the next tier.**

This single metric captures:
- Acquisition (they found us and paid)
- Quality (they completed the material)
- Satisfaction (they came back for more)

When this number grows, everything else follows: revenue, reputation, referrals, and community.
