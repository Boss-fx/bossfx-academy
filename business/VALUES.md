# Company Values — BossFx Academy

> **Last Updated:** 2026-06-30
> **Owner:** CEO

---

## 1. Honesty Over Hype

We never promise unrealistic returns. We show validated results (MetaQuotes verification, real curriculum, real student outcomes). Our brand earns trust by being transparent about what trading is: a skill that requires discipline, education, and time.

**In practice:** No "10x your money" claims. No fake testimonials. No rented lifestyle content. Product pages describe exactly what the student gets.

## 2. Revenue First

Every decision is filtered through: does this protect or grow revenue? The payment flow is sacred. Analytics tracking is non-negotiable. Anything that risks a customer's ability to pay, receive their product, or access their downloads is treated as a P0 incident.

**In practice:** See [sop/incident-response.md](/sop/incident-response.md) — payment failures are highest severity.

## 3. Build to Last

We don't build throwaway prototypes. Every system is documented, every automation is mapped, every integration is tested. The repository is the operating system of the company — it should work without the founder for weeks at a time.

**In practice:** CLAUDE.md, PROJECT_ROADMAP.md, AUTOMATION_MAP.md, and 26 documentation files ensure any engineer (human or AI) can understand and operate the platform.

## 4. Africa is Home

We build for African traders. Naira payments. Lagos timezone for cron jobs. Content that references local trading conditions. Community on Telegram (not Discord or Slack) because that's where Nigerian traders already are.

**In practice:** Flutterwave NGN, Africa/Lagos timezone defaults, Telegram community links in every email and product page.

## 5. Automate Everything

If a human does it more than twice, it should be automated. Lead capture, email drips, payment fulfillment, download delivery, re-engagement — all automated. The founder's time should go to content, community, and strategy, not manual order processing.

**In practice:** See [AUTOMATION_MAP.md](/AUTOMATION_MAP.md) — 11 active automated workflows.

## 6. Protect the Student

Every decision about pricing, content, and support should serve the student's learning outcome. Refund policy exists and is fair. Course content is substantive. Mentorship includes real access to the founder. Downloads work. Emails arrive.

**In practice:** Token-gated downloads with audit trails, ICS calendar invites for mentorship, admin dashboard for resending failed emails.
