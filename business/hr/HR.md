## Burnout Prevention

Solo founder risk is real. Guard against burnout:

1. **Set boundaries** — No work after 9 PM
2. **Batch tasks** — Content days, dev days, admin days
3. **Automate relentlessly** — Every manual task is a future automation
4. **Take Sundays off** — Rest is productive
5. **Celebrate wins** — Track milestones, not just tasks
6. **Review quarterly** — Am I working on the right things?
