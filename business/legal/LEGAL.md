## Content Piracy Protection

### Current Protections
- Token-gated downloads with HMAC-SHA256 signatures
- Time-expiring download links (72h standard, 720h VIP)
- Download audit trail (IP, user agent, timestamp)
- /downloads/ path blocked by robots.txt and vercel.json 404 route

### If Piracy Detected
1. Document the infringement (screenshots, URLs)
2. DMCA takedown notice to hosting provider
3. Contact platform support if on social media
4. Consider watermarking future content

## Regulatory Considerations

### Forex Education in Nigeria
- Forex trading is legal in Nigeria
- Education about forex is legal
- No specific license required for forex education
- Must not provide financial advice or manage client funds
- Must include risk disclaimers

### Consumer Protection
- Products must be delivered as described
- Refund policy must be clear
- No false advertising or income guarantees
- Distance selling regulations apply (digital goods)
