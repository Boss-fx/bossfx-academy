## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars
1. **Trading education** — Fundamentals, strategies, analysis (60% of content)
2. **Results & proof** — Trade results, student wins, EA performance (20%)
3. **Tools & automation** — EA features, resource tools, tech stack (10%)
4. **Lifestyle & motivation** — Trader lifestyle, mindset, discipline (10%)

### Content Calendar Framework

| Day | Instagram | TikTok | X | Blog |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Trading tip (carousel) | Quick tip (60s) | Market open commentary | — |
| Tuesday | EA showcase / result | EA in action | Trading thread | — |

| Thursday | Student success / testimonial | Reaction/trend | Market analysis | — |
| Friday | Weekend prep / mindset | Quick tip | Trading reflection | — |

### Content Production Process
1. **Batch create** — Produce 5-7 social posts in one session
2. **Repurpose** — Every long-form piece becomes 3-5 short-form pieces
3. **Schedule** — Use native scheduling tools on each platform
4. **Engage** — Respond to comments/DMs within 24 hours
5. **Analyze** — Monthly content performance review

## Email Marketing

### Current Email System
- **Platform:** Brevo
- **Lists:** General (2), Webinar (3), Mentorship (5), Resource (6)
- **Drip sequences:** 6 active (welcome, webinar, resource, mentorship, exit_intent, reengagement)
- **Daily cron:** Processes pending drip steps at 09:00 UTC

### Email Strategy
1. **Capture everywhere** — Every page should have a lead capture point
2. **Segment by intent** — Different sequences for different entry points
3. **Score leads** — 5-40 points based on action (mentorship inquiry = highest)
4. **Nurture to purchase** — Each sequence drives toward a product CTA
5. **Re-engage inactive** — 30-day inactive contacts get win-back sequence

### Known Issue
Newsletter form submissions don't reach Brevo (config.js `emailProvider: 'none'`). This is a Phase 1 fix priority.

## Campaign Types

### Launch Campaign (New Product)
See [/sop/new-product-launch.md](/sop/new-product-launch.md)
1. Teaser content (1 week before)
2. Launch announcement (all channels)
3. Email blast to relevant list
4. Launch-week pricing or bonus
5. Social proof collection

### Promotional Campaign (Existing Product)
1. Identify underperforming product
2. Create focused content series (3-5 posts)
3. Email sequence to relevant segment
4. Limited-time offer or bonus
5. Measure conversion lift

### Content Campaign (Awareness)
1. Identify trending topic or keyword opportunity
2. Create pillar content (blog post or video)
3. Break into 5-7 social media pieces
4. Distribute over 1-2 weeks
5. Capture leads from engagement
