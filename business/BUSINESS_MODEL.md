## Pricing Strategy

### Current Approach
**Value ladder:** Courses → Group mentorship → 1-on-1 mentorship → VIP lifetime

Each product is priced to be accessible to Nigerian traders while maintaining high margins. The EA addon (₦15K) is positioned as an impulse upsell during checkout.

### Pricing Anchors
- Forex 101 at ₦25,000 is positioned against informal WhatsApp courses (₦5K-₦15K) as a premium but structured alternative
- VIP at ₦350,000 is the anchor that makes mentorship (₦60K-₦150K) feel reasonable
- EA at ₦15,000 is positioned against MQL5 market EAs ($50-$500)

## Key Business Constraints

| Constraint | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Founder is sole operator | Bottleneck on content, sales, support | Automate everything possible, document SOPs |
| NGN-only payments | Limits international customers | Multi-currency planned (Phase 4) |
| Free tier infrastructure | Service limits (12 functions, 300 emails/day) | Upgrade when revenue justifies it |
| No recurring billing | Mentorship renewals are manual | Flutterwave subscription API (Phase 4) |
| Single market (Nigeria) | Revenue ceiling | Regional expansion planned (Phase 4) |
