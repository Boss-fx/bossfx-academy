## Competitive Position

**Differentiators:**
1. MetaQuotes-validated EA — not just another "forex guru" with no proof
2. African-first — built for Nigerian traders in Naira, understanding local payment methods
3. Full-stack digital delivery — no WhatsApp PDFs or Google Drive links; professional token-gated downloads
4. Education + Tools — course + EA + community, not just one product

**Market:** Nigeria's forex trading population is estimated at 1.3M+ active traders (2025). Most education is informal (WhatsApp groups, Instagram). Professional, structured platforms are rare.
