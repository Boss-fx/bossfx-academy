Pre-Trade Checklist

The exact checklist BossFx mentors use before every trade. Never skip a step.

Print this checklist and go through it before every single trade. Consistency in your process leads to consistency in your results.

Phase 1: Market Context

Before you even look at your chart
✓
Check the economic calendarAre there high-impact news events in the next 2 hours? If yes, stay out or reduce size.
✓
Identify the current market sessionLondon/NY overlap = high volatility. Asian session = range-bound. Trade accordingly.
✓
Check daily bias from higher timeframeWhat's the Daily and H4 trend? Your trades should align with this direction.
✓
Confirm you are emotionally neutralAre you angry, excited, revenge trading, or bored? If yes — do NOT trade.

Phase 2: Technical Analysis

Chart analysis before entry
✓
Mark key support and resistance levelsUse H4/Daily levels. These are your decision zones.
✓
Identify market structureIs price making higher highs/lows (uptrend) or lower highs/lows (downtrend)?
✓
Wait for your entry signalDoes your specific strategy give you a clear signal? No signal = no trade.
✓
Confirm with at least one additional indicatorRSI divergence, volume confirmation, or candlestick pattern at your level.

Phase 3: Risk Management

Protect your capital first
✓
Calculate position sizeRisk no more than 1-2% of your account. Use a position size calculator.
✓
Set your stop loss BEFORE enteringKnow exactly where your SL goes. Never enter without one.
✓
Verify risk-to-reward is at least 1:2If risking 30 pips, your TP should be at least 60 pips away.
✓
Check daily loss limitHave you lost 3%+ today? If yes, stop trading for the day.

Phase 4: Execution

Pull the trigger with confidence
✓
Enter the trade with SL and TP setAll three should be placed at the same time. Not after.
✓
Log the trade in your journalRecord: pair, direction, entry, SL, TP, lot size, and your reasoning.
✓
Step away from the screenSet alerts. Don't stare at the trade. Let your plan work.
BossFx Rule If you can't check every box on this list, you don't take the trade. Discipline is the edge. The market will always be there tomorrow.
