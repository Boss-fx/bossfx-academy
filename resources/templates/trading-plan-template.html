05 Daily Routine

Pre-Market (Before First Trade)

Check economic calendar for high-impact events
Mark key support and resistance on higher timeframe
Identify daily bias (bullish, bearish, or range)
Confirm emotional state — am I neutral and focused?
Review yesterday's trades in journal

During Session

Follow entry rules — no improvisations
Set SL and TP before entering every trade
Log every trade immediately in journal
Stop after max consecutive losses

Post-Market

Review all trades taken today
Screenshot best and worst trade for review
Note one lesson learned
Plan tomorrow's watchlist

06 When I Will NOT Trade

Know your "no trade" triggers
I've hit my daily loss limit
I'm emotionally compromised (angry, anxious, euphoric)
High-impact news within 30 minutes
I don't have a clear setup from my strategy
Market is choppy with no clear structure
I'm revenge trading after a loss
I'm bored and looking for action
BossFx RulePrint this plan and keep it next to your trading screen. Before every session, read sections 03 and 06. The traders who stick to their plan are the ones still trading a year from now.
