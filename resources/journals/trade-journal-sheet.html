Trade Journal Sheet

Track every trade. Review every week. Improve every month. The journal is your real edge.

By Timilehin 'BossFx' Shobande — Founder, BossFx Academy

01 Why Journaling Wins

Your journal is the most powerful tool you own. It reveals patterns in your behavior — which setups you actually profit from, what time of day you overtrade, and when emotion takes over. Without a journal, you're guessing. With one, you're using data.

  • Identify your best setups — Know which strategies actually make you money
  • Spot emotional patterns — See when revenge trading or FOMO costs you
  • Track your real win rate — Not what you think it is, what it actually is
  • Measure improvement — Compare this month to last month with hard numbers
