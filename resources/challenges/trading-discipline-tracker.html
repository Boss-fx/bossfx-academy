30-Day Trading Discipline Tracker

Build unbreakable trading habits in 30 days. Track your discipline, not just your profits.

By Timilehin 'BossFx' Shobande — Founder, BossFx Academy

01 The Challenge

For the next 30 trading days, your only goal is discipline. Not profits, not pips, not win rate — pure process discipline. Follow 6 simple rules every trading day and track your score.

Research shows it takes 21-30 days to form a habit. By the end of this challenge, disciplined trading will be your default mode.

02 The 6 Daily Rules

1Pre-trade checklist completed

Go through your full checklist before any trade

2Risk ≤ 1% per trade

Position size calculated correctly for every entry

3Stop loss set before entry

SL placed at the same time as your entry order

4R:R ≥ 1:2 on all trades

No trades where the target is less than 2x the risk

5All trades journaled

Every trade logged with entry, exit, and notes

6No revenge or impulse trades

Every trade came from your strategy, not emotion

Each rule is worth 1 point per day. A perfect day = 6/6. If you didn't trade that day (because there was no setup), you get a full 6/6 — sitting out IS discipline.

05 Weekly Reflection

At the end of each week, answer these honestly:

  1. Which rule did I break most often? Why?
  2. What triggered my worst trading decisions?
  3. Did I trade when I should have sat out?
  4. What's one specific thing I'll improve next week?
