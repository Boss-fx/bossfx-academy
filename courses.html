Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need any trading experience?
Not at all. Forex 101 is built from the ground up for complete beginners. We start with the absolute basics — what forex is, how currencies work — and build up from there. Zero prior knowledge required.
What platform do I need?
We teach using MetaTrader 4/5 and TradingView — both are free to use. The course includes full training on setting up and navigating both platforms so you're ready from day one.
How long does it take to complete?
The course is self-paced. Most students complete Forex 101 in 4-6 weeks when dedicating a few hours per week. You get lifetime access, so there's no rush — learn at your own pace.
Do I get community access?
Yes! Every Forex 101 student gets access to our private trading community. Share your analysis, get feedback, join live sessions, and connect with fellow traders on the same journey.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
We stand behind our education. If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days, contact us and we'll work with you. Our goal is your success — we want every student to get real value.
